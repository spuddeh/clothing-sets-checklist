# Clothing Sets Checklist: Locations Guide

> Every location, requirement, and walking direction from the in-game checklist, in one written record.

This is a companion article to the [Clothing Sets Checklist](https://www.nexusmods.com/cyberpunk2077/mods/26829) mod. Every entry below is generated from the same data the in-game UI uses, so it stays consistent with what the mod tells you. Use it as a written reference if you do not have Cyber Engine Tweaks installed, can't install mods (console), or just prefer reading.

If you spot anything inaccurate, please leave a comment on the mod page so it can be fixed in both the article and the in-game database.

---

## How to read this guide

- Items include the nearest fast travel point, district, any requirement, and walking directions.
- Where an entry has a short summary and detailed steps, the summary is the first line and the detailed steps follow.
- Life path starting outfits and ending epilogue clothing are intentionally excluded.

## Badge

### Holo-tinted badge goggles

- **Fast Travel:** Northside Apartment
- **Location:** Watson, Northside

Looted from a deceased cop behind the Northside apartment.

From the Northside Apartment fast travel terminal, head west along the street and cross the Goldsmith and Martin intersection turning right and follow the road until you reach a small alley on the left. Head down the alley until you get to the open space, there should be an area with an NCPD squad car on your left.
The body is next to the car.

### Heavy-duty aramid-reinforced badge coat

- **Fast Travel:** Almuneca & Jerez
- **Location:** Santo Domingo, Rancho Coronado

Looted off a corpse at what appears to be a failed attempt to apprehend a criminal at a house near the southern edge of Rancho Coronado.

Facing the Almuneca & Jerez fast travel terminal, look West North West. You should see a NCPD SUV. Head towards it.
The body is next to the furthest NCPD squad car.

### Anti-puncture neotac pants with composite lining

- **Fast Travel:** Metro: Farrier & Ferguson
- **Location:** Watson, Little China

Looted from a deceased cop in a recessed walkway near a side road on the northwest side of Little China.

From the metro, head west until you see a blue neon 24 arrow pointing to the location with the body.
Just look for the blood.

### Waterproof badge combat boots

- **Fast Travel:** Woodhaven Street
- **Location:** Santo Domingo, Rancho Coronado

You can loot two pairs of boots from a couple of formerly living NCPD Badges near a pile of garbage at the base of the Petrochem Dam.

From the Woodhaven Street fast travel terminal, head west until see 3 sets of stairs leading up. At the top of the 3rd set you'll see a building with a pinky-purple door right in front of you.
The bodies are on the roof.

## Corporate

### Tactical hybrid-glass corporate glasses

- **Fast Travel:** Rancho Coronado East
- **Location:** Santo Domingo, Rancho Coronado

Looted from a metal chest at a billboard near a bend in the road.

From Rancho Coronado East fast travel, head south-west along the winding road leading up and away from Rancho Coronado.
Look for the billboard structure near the northernmost bend.

### Corporate blazer with bulletproof lining

- **Fast Travel:** Offshore St
- **Location:** Watson, Northside

Looted from a corpse next to a burning car and a closed diner.

From the Offshore St Fast travel head north east across the train tracks and turn left. Follow the road until you get to an intersection and turn right. Keep following that road until you get to a building with a 'Barely Illegal' sign out the front on your right.
The body is next to the burning car nearby.

### Carbonweave silk corporate shirt

- **Fast Travel:** Corporation St
- **Location:** City Center, Downtown

Looted from a body behind scaffolding in a back alley.

From Corporation St fast travel, cross the road and head east until you get to a construction site on your right.
Go under the scaffolding to find the body.

### Breathable reinforced bio-cotton corporate slacks

- **Fast Travel:** Oil Fields
- **Location:** Watson, Northside

Looted from a corpse next to a burning car and a Trauma Team AV.

From the Oil Fields fast travel, follow the road  south-west and turn left at the intersection. Cross the bridge and turn left at the next intersection Head towards the building on your left. You will see a Trauma Team AV and a burning car in the parking lot.
The body is next to the burning car.

### Ergonomic reinforced corporate pumps/evening shoes

- **Requirement:** Complete 'I fought the Law' side job
- **Fast Travel:** Sam Amaro St
- **Location:** Westbrook, Charter Hill

Looted from a body between burning vans.

From Sam Amaro St fast travel, head north across the bridge and turn left, then immediately take the next right. Enter the parking lot of the large building past the boom gates.
The body to the right near the burning vans.

## Fixer

### Polycarbonate opti-enhanced fixer glasses

- **Fast Travel:** Rancho Coronado South
- **Location:** Santo Domingo, Arroyo

Looted from a dead body in an alley.

From the Rancho Coronado South fast travel, head south-west. Look for an industrial site full of containers and a big crane at the end of the T-intersection.
The body is behind the blue shipping containers.

### Trilayer aramid-weave fixer skirt with jacket/coat

- **Fast Travel:** Kendal Park
- **Location:** Santo Domingo, Rancho Coronado

Looted from a box in the middle of the destroyed 'Ferris wheel' area.

From Kendal Park fast travel, head to the destroyed Ferris wheel.
The box is right in front of one of the capsules.

### Heat-resistant aramid-weave fixer bustier/shirt

- **Fast Travel:** Ebunike metro station
- **Location:** Heywood, The Glen

Looted from a corpse in a back alley.

From Ebunike metro station, turn right and follow the road south. Take a left at the intersection onto the one way street. There is a green metal wall with Valentino's graffiti next to a Buck-A-Slice on your right at the corner of Haakenson and Piper. Jump over the wall to the other side.
If you don't have double jump yet, you can use the nearby vendor tent to jump over the fence.

### Protective-layer fixer skirt/pants

- **Fast Travel:** Jig-jig Street
- **Location:** Westbrook, Japantown

Looted from a small hut on the roof of a building.

From the fast travel terminal, face the Jig-Jig Street purple sign. Look up to your left and climb up until you reach a metal shack on the roof of that building.
The item is inside the shack.

### Elastiweave fixer pumps with reinforced seams/shoes

- **Fast Travel:** Crescent & Broad St
- **Location:** Westbrook, Japantown

Looted from a dead man's body in a blue crashed van (he is the driver).

Face the Crescent & Broad St fast travel terminal and look left. There is a ramp leading to an underground garage.
The blue van is on the ramp.

### Laminate-armor media ballistic vest

- **Fast Travel:** Crescent & Broad St
- **Location:** Westbrook, Japantown

Looted from a dead body on a balcony.

Face the Crescent & Broad St fast travel terminal, turn right and diagonally cross intersection. Take a right turn again and enter the alley with the Xiao Xiongmao restaurant. Climb the stairs at the end of the alley. Look at the building on the right with the balconies.

### Heat-resistant nanoweave media shirt

- **Fast Travel:** Corporation St
- **Location:** Heywood, Wellsprings

Looted from under the street jutting out into the water.

From Corporation St fast travel, look for the street extending over the water and follow it towards the end. As you head down the street there will be multiple seating areas on the left and right. When you get to the last one on the right, take the stairs down, then climb over the fence to access this area directly below the street.

### Anti-piercing tactical media cargo pants

- **Fast Travel:** San Amaro St
- **Location:** Westbrook, Charter Hill

Looted from dead body on a platform with an billboard.

From San Amaro St fast travel, head north across the river and turn left. Follow the street until you get to the second street on the right and turn down it. You should see an billboard on the right.

### Lightweight hardened-rubber media steel-toes

- **Fast Travel:** Lele Park
- **Location:** Westbrook, Charter Hill

The body is on the top of the food vendor. You can easily climb up using the umbrellas in front of the restaurant.
Potentially contains the pants as well as the shoes.

## Netrunner

### Antisurge ICE-protected netrunner infovisor

- **Requirement:** Reported Crime: Death from Above
- **Fast Travel:** Abandoned Fuel Station
- **Location:** Badlands, Jackson Plains

Looted from a metal container at the end of Reported Crime: Death from Above.

From Abandoned Fuel Station fast travel, head east along the road to find a burned car with a corpse. Loot it and read the shard to start the mission. The location is a little to the northwest from the fast travel terminal.
It's possible to get 2 of the Netrunner infovisors from the container.

### Heat-resistant hybridweave netrunning suit

- **Fast Travel:** Ventura & Skyline
- **Location:** Heywood, Wellsprings

Looted from a body in a small metal hut in The Glen, in Heywood.

Facing the Ventura & Skyline fast terminal, turn left and head down the road. Cross over the streat heading right and continue straight You will see a ramp leading into an alcove on your right. Directly ahead is a metal shack with big orange pipe going along on its right side. Go to the back of the shack and find the hole in the bottom.
Crouch to enter and head to the second room, where you will find a corpse with this item.

### Dura-membrane netrunner neotac pants

- **Fast Travel:** Perishing Street
- **Location:** Watson, Northside

Looted from a dead netrunner at the top of a water tower.

Facing The Perishing Street fast travel terminal, turn right and follow the street and take the next left up the ramp to the parking lot. On your right there are two identical water towers; you want the northern most one, behind the building. There will be a motorcycle at the bottom of the stairs.
Climb to the top to find the corpse with the loot.

### Hardened netrunner boots with composite inserts

- **Fast Travel:** Luxury Apartments
- **Location:** Westbrook, Charter Hill

Found on a body inside a shaft in Charter Hill, Westbrook.

Facing the Luxury Apartments fast travel terminal turn right and head down the street. Take a right at the intersection. From there head south-west towards Lele St. When you get to the next intersection look diagonal left and cross over the street. There's an open hatch over the railings with another body just outside it.
Head inside the hatch and find the body with the loot.

## Solo

### Titanium solo techgogs with tactical software

- **Fast Travel:** Protein Farm
- **Location:** Badlands, Biotechnica Flats

Looted from a dead body in the shipping landmass.

From Protein Farm fast travel, head east (or west from Fuel Station).
The body is at a blocked off T-intersection halfway between the two fast travel terminals on the map.

### Heavy shock-absorbent solo jacket

- **Requirement:** Technical 20
- **Fast Travel:** Senate & Market
- **Location:** Heywood, The Glen

Looted from a small caged area.

From Senate & Market fast travel terminal, cross the road and head east past the Time Machine music shop. Keep following the road east to towards Megabuilding 01. On the right side of Megabuilding 01, there is an alley with a metal cage on the left. The only entrance is through the door. You need 20 Technical to open the door.
As you enter, keep walking to the other end of the cage, the body will be on your left.

### Ultrathin composite-print solo shirt

- **Fast Travel:** Old Turbines
- **Location:** Badlands, Jackson Plains

Looted from a suitcase next to a small rock formation.

From the Old Turbines fast travel terminal, head east-north-east. Cross over the road and continue until you see a small rock formation with some bodies and look scattered around.
The item is in the suitcase

### Armor-plated syn-leather solo pants

- **Fast Travel:** Pumping Station
- **Location:** Heywood, Wellsprings

Looted from a body in a shack in an alcove on the beach.

From Pumping Station fast travel, head west until you reach the end of the street. Enter the balcony area through the open gate. Follow that around to the left and you should find a lader on your right. Take that down to the beach. Immediatly turn left and walk south keeping an eye on your left for a green metal fence. Through the fence is a shack, jump over it to find the entrance.
The body with the pants is inside that shack on a corpse of an Animal gang member NPC.

### Gold-tipped manganese steel solo boots

- **Fast Travel:** Kabuki
- **Location:** Watson, Kabuki

Looted from a metal container behind a cook.

From Kabuki fast travel terminal, head south-west and cross the road. You should see an entrance with stairs leading down to the markets. Turn left after the stairs and take the elevator to the 3rd floor.
The container is behind the cook.

## Patch Quests

### David's Jacket

- **Requirement:** Cyberpunk 2077 Patch 1.6 or newer
- **Fast Travel:** Megabuilding H4
- **Location:** Santo Domingo, Arroyo

Reward from 'Over the Edge' side job.

Facing the Megabuilding H4 fast travel terminal, cut through the parking lot on your left and turn right on to the road. Follow the road past the first intersection and then take the second intersection on your right. Follow the street around the bend and you will find an alcove filled with trash and the Edgerunner's logo.
Interacte with the BD wreath in the trash can to begin the quest.

### The Joker of Night City (Masc V)

- **Requirement:** Cyberpunk 2077 Patch 2.2 or newer

Can be obtained after collecting all 10 Joker shards scattered throughout Night City.
The t-shirt can be picked up next to the final shard.

### The Joker of Night City (Fem V)

- **Requirement:** Cyberpunk 2077 Patch 2.2 or newer

Can be obtained after collecting all 10 Joker shards scattered throughout Night City.
The t-shirt can be picked up next to the final shard.
